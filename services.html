What We Do

OUR ELECTRICAL SERVICES

From a single outlet to a full commercial build-out, our licensed team handles every scope of electrical work — safely, on time, and within budget.

🏠

Residential Services

Everything your home needs — done right and permitted.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Upgrade your outdated breaker panel to handle modern electrical loads safely and reliably.

  • 100A, 150A & 200A upgrades
  • Federal Pacific / Zinsco replacements
  • Full permit & inspection included
  • Utility coordination handled

Whole-Home Rewiring

Replace aging or aluminum wiring with modern copper wiring for safety and code compliance.

  • Aluminum-to-copper conversions
  • Knob-and-tube replacement
  • Minimal drywall damage approach
  • Full code compliance

Outlet & Switch Installation

Add, replace, or upgrade outlets and switches throughout your home.

  • GFCI & AFCI protection
  • USB outlet installation
  • Smart switch compatibility
  • Outdoor & weatherproof outlets

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, ceiling fans, under-cabinet lighting, and full home automation tie-ins.

  • LED recessed lighting
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Dimmer & 3-way switches
  • Exterior & landscape lighting

EV Charger Installation

Certified installation of Level 2 home EV chargers for all major vehicle makes.

  • Tesla, ChargePoint & more
  • Dedicated 240V circuit
  • Garage & exterior mounting
  • Rebate paperwork assistance

Generator Installation

Whole-home or partial-home standby generator installation with automatic transfer switch.

  • Generac & Kohler certified
  • Automatic transfer switches
  • Natural gas & propane hookup
  • Annual maintenance plans

Commercial Services

Tenant Build-Outs

Commercial Lighting

  • LED conversion & retrofits

  • Emergency & exit lighting
