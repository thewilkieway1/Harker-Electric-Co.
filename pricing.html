Questions

PRICING FAQ

Do you charge for estimates?
No. All in-home estimates for residential projects are free. For large commercial scopes, we may charge a design fee which is credited back if you hire us.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Financing options are available through GreenSky for projects over $2,500.
Is the permit fee included in your quote?
Yes. On all projects requiring a permit, the permit cost is included in your flat-rate quote. No surprise permit fees at the end.
What if the job takes longer than expected?
Our pricing is flat-rate, not hourly. As long as the scope doesn't change, the price doesn't change — even if it takes us longer than expected.
Do you offer senior or military discounts?
Yes — we offer a 10% discount for active military, veterans, and seniors 65+. Just mention it when you call or submit your estimate request.
