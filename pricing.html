Pricing FAQs

COST QUESTIONS
ANSWERED.

How much does an electrician charge per hour in Port St. Lucie, FL?

Licensed electricians in Port St. Lucie typically charge between $85 and $150 per hour depending on the type of work and time of day. Our standard service call is $99 and includes the first hour of diagnosis and labor. For larger jobs, we quote flat-rate pricing so you're never watching the clock — you know the cost before we start.

How much does an electrical panel upgrade cost in Port St. Lucie?

A 200-amp electrical panel upgrade in Port St. Lucie typically costs between $1,800 and $3,500. The range depends on the age and condition of the existing panel, accessibility, utility coordination, and any code corrections required. All permits and the final county inspection are included in our quoted price — no extras.

How much does it cost to rewire a house in Port St. Lucie?

Whole home rewiring in Port St. Lucie, FL generally costs $8,000 to $20,000+ depending on the home's square footage, age, number of circuits, and how accessible the walls and attic are. A typical 1,500 sq ft single-story home averages $8,000–$12,000 including permit and inspection. We provide free on-site estimates with no obligation.

What does an electrical inspection cost near me?

A residential electrical safety inspection from Voltex Electric starts at $149 for homes under 2,000 sq ft. The inspection covers your main panel, visible wiring, outlets, GFCI and AFCI compliance, grounding, and smoke/CO detector placement. You'll receive a detailed written report — useful for real estate transactions, insurance, or peace of mind.

How much does it cost to install a ceiling fan in Port St. Lucie?

Ceiling fan installation costs $125–$250 per fan if an existing ceiling box is in place. If new wiring must be run from the panel, costs increase to $250–$450 per fan. We can also install a new wall switch, dimmer, or remote receiver at the same time. We install all brands — just supply the fan or let us recommend one.

Are your prices final, or will there be add-ons?

We provide flat-rate quotes in writing before any work begins. If our technician discovers something unexpected during the job (it happens in older homes), we stop and explain the situation before proceeding. You always approve any additional costs before they're incurred — no surprises on the invoice.

Does Voltex Electric offer any discounts?

Yes — we offer a 10% senior discount for homeowners 65+, and a $50 referral credit for customers who send us a new client. We also run seasonal promotions on panel upgrades and EV charger installations. Call or email us to ask about current offers.
