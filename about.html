Our Story

LOCAL ROOTS.
EXPERT HANDS.

Voltex Electric Co. was founded in 2012 by Marcus Voltaire, a third-generation electrician who grew up in St. Lucie County. After spending eight years working for large regional contractors, Marcus started Voltex with a simple goal: give Treasure Coast homeowners access to a licensed, insured electrician in Port St. Lucie who actually shows up on time, explains the work clearly, and charges fairly.

Over the past 12 years, we've grown from a one-truck operation to a team of eight licensed technicians — but we've stayed intentionally local. We don't subcontract work, we don't upsell, and we don't send a salesperson to your house. When you call Voltex, you get a certified electrician with Port St. Lucie roots who treats your home like their own.

We serve homeowners, landlords, real estate investors, and small businesses across Port St. Lucie, Fort Pierce, Vero Beach, Stuart, and Jensen Beach. Every job — from a single outlet repair to a whole-home rewire — gets the same level of care and attention.

FROM YOUR ELECTRICIAN.
