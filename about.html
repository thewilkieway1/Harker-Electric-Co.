Our Story

BUILT WITH HARD WORK

Family-owned and operated since 2004, Harker Electric has grown from a one-truck operation to one of Central Florida's most trusted electrical contractors.

Who We Are

TWO DECADES IN THE FIELD

Harker Electric was founded in 2004 by Dale Harker, a master electrician who spent a decade working for larger contractors before deciding he could do it better — with more honesty, tighter craftsmanship, and a real commitment to the customer.

Starting with a single service van and a handful of residential clients in Orlando, Dale built the company job by job, referral by referral. By 2010, the crew had grown to eight technicians and the company had expanded into commercial work.

Today, Harker Electric employs 22 full-time licensed electricians and serves hundreds of homes and businesses across Central Florida. Dale still walks job sites and personally reviews every major estimate. That hasn't changed.
